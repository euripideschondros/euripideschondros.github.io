About MEK Hair Studio

MEK Hair Studio is a family owned salon dedicated to providing clients with the highest quality products and services for your expert hair and beauty care. With over ten years experience in the hair industry, sisters Irene and Catherine, from Kavala in Greece, are delighted to now be serving clients in Marston Green and Greater Birmingham.

Services

Cuts

MEK Hair Studio offer high-quality haircuts for both adults and children from highly trained, professional stylists. You'll be sure to get an up-to-date, on-trend style that's right for you.

Treatment

Treatments to hair can change the way hair looks and feels. chemical relaxant to curly hair making it smooth and straight. Chemical perms cause a semi-permanent style such as curls or waves in otherwise straight hair.
